Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026 · SM Digital Solutions

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to SM Stock Alerts, we collect:

  • Phone number — Required to deliver SMS alerts
  • Name — Optional, used for personalization
  • Email address — Optional, used for scanner waitlist and updates
  • Signup source — How you found us (landing page, TikTok, etc.)
  • Signup timestamp — Date and time of subscription for compliance records

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver daily stock alert SMS messages
  • To send welcome and service-related messages
  • To maintain subscriber records for regulatory compliance
  • To improve our service and alert quality

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Twilio — Our SMS delivery provider, solely to send messages to your phone
  • Law enforcement — Only when required by law or valid legal process

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted databases hosted on Render. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe: Reply STOP to any SMS to immediately unsubscribe
  • Data deletion: Contact us to request deletion of all your data
  • Access: Contact us to request a copy of the data we hold about you

6. SMS Consent Records

We maintain records of your subscription consent, including your phone number, the date and time you subscribed, and the source of your signup. These records are kept for compliance with telecommunications regulations.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our landing page does not use cookies or tracking pixels. We do not track your browsing activity.

8. Children's Privacy

SM Stock Alerts is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact SM Digital Solutions at thetradingprotocol@gmail.com
